The box for the Moon

They say this box was left behind by someone
who couldn’t say everything when it mattered most.
So they carved it into puzzles.
Sealed it with time.
Waiting - not for the right answer,
but for the right person to open it.

Now it’s yours. But only if you can trace the path she always walked.

Congratulations

Hello Moon

Sometimes it takes too many riddles to realize
the answer was to look inward all along.
Congratulations on completing the puzzle.
I leave you with a short poem — just for you:

Sometimes moon wanes.
Sometimes she hides.
But she always has
a grip on the sea
And so does on me
